Hello, this is Nichos Molnar

and this is a little bit about me.

Right now, I'm working on my new year's resolution: draw the 14 tallest mountains in the world.

Around Christmastime, I finished Into Thin Air, an account of a mountaneering disaster on the slopes of Mount Everest. And after reading about the loss of 12 lives on the mountain in one terrible season, I wanted nothing more than to get as close to the mountain as I could. I sent myself down a rabbit hole of research which dominated my thinking so much that my mountain fascinatination (and the book) are mentioned prominently in my application to this program.

Coincidentally, I was learning how to draw, and was looking for new subjects. As it turns out, Mountains are good subject material (and pretty easy to learn how to draw, to boot). So I set my goal: 14 mountains, 12 months. Something achievable.
